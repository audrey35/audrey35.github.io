Plant Care

A MERN website

View Plant Care (Heroku) View Code (GitHub)

Plant Care website is for beginners at raising plants that want reliable, non- conflicting advice on how to raise plants.

The site provides a forum where the person can ask questions the come up while raising plants. Users can also search for general information and guides about the plants using the plant search tool. The internet has countless number of advice for people who are new at the art of raising healthy plants, but many of these advice are contradictory, which can be very confusing for beginners to navigate. For example, wilting plants could be an indication of overwatering the plant, but it could also be a sign that the plant needs water. The Plant Care website aims to provide a reliable source of information for beginners who are having trouble correctly identifying the problem that their plant is experiencing and accurately addressing the problem.
